Poorvi Enchanting

Poorvi Enchanting, developed by MSR Shelters, is a residential project that promises modern living with a blend of luxury and convenience. However, the project has faced several challenges that have impacted its reputation among residents.

Highlights

  • Spacious apartments with modern designs.
  • Proximity to schools, hospitals, and shopping centers.
  • Well-maintained green spaces and recreational areas.
  • 24/7 security and power backup.

Issues

  • Electricity issues leading to frequent power outages.
  • Inconsistent water supply in taps, kitchen, and flush.
  • Security guidelines are not visibly pasted for residents and visitors.
  • No solution provided yet for the car wash facility.
  • Water logging in common areas, including the cricket ground, even after window installation.
  • Seepage in utility areas, common areas, and basement parking.
  • Water leakage from bathroom guns.
  • Pending installation of flat number boards.
  • Lift lobby floor number relocation remains incomplete.
  • Car parking allocation disputes among residents.
  • Need for regular mosquito fogging to prevent health issues.
  • STP water softener concerns affecting water quality.
  • Occupancy Certificate (OC) status remains unclear.
  • Pending keys handover for several flats.
  • DTH setup issues causing inconvenience to residents.

Construction

The construction quality of the apartments has been a major concern. Below are some of the key issues:

  • Build Quality: Very Poor
  • Build Materials: The lowest level of materials have been used to build the apartments. Some highlights include:
    • Sanitary Pipes: Sudhakar Pipes
    • Tiles & Putty: The tiles have been installed with very poor skills, causing water to stagnate in multiple areas.
    • Construction Material: Lower-grade materials have been used.
    • Doors: Very poor installation quality.
